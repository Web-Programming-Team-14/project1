Heat the Pan
Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Add a small amount of butter or oil to coat the bottom of the pan.
Mix the Eggs with Condiments
Crack the egg(s) into a small bowl. Add a pinch of salt and pepper. If using any additional herbs or spices, add them now. Gently mix.
Start Frying
Once the pan is hot, carefully pour the egg mixture into the pan. Cook until the white is set and the yolk reaches your desired consistency (about 2-3 minutes for over-easy, 3-4 minutes for over-medium).
Serve and Enjoy
Carefully slide the egg onto a plate. Serve immediately with toast, bacon, or your preferred sides.
Ingredients
Eggs (1-2 per serving)
Salt (to taste)
Black pepper (to taste)
Butter or oil for frying
Optional: Herbs or spices (e.g., paprika, chives)
Optional: Toast, bacon, or your preferred sides
